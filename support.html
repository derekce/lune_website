Frequently asked questions

What does Luna do?

Luna shows you the current moon phase, counts down to the next full moon, and offers a daily affirmation. You can set personal intentions for each half of the lunar cycle, save moon events to your calendar, and add a home-screen widget.

Why does Luna ask for my location?

Only to show local weather next to the moon. If you'd rather not share it, decline the permission — everything else in the app still works. You can change this anytime in your device's Settings under Luna.

Does Luna need an internet connection?

No. Moon phases are calculated right on your device, so the core app works fully offline. An internet connection is only used to fetch current weather.

How do I turn the cycle reminders on or off?

Reminders follow your notification permission. To stop them, turn off notifications for Luna in your device's Settings. (A built-in toggle is on our roadmap.)

The home-screen widget looks wrong or won't update.

Try removing and re-adding the widget. Widgets refresh periodically rather than instantly, so give it a little time. If it still looks off, email us with a screenshot.

Do you collect any of my data?

No. Luna has no accounts, no analytics, and no trackers. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

How do I delete my data?

All your data lives on your device. Deleting the app removes it. You can also clear saved content from within the app or your device settings.
